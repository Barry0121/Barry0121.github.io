Hi! My name is Zexin Xue. Feel free to call me Barry.

I am currently studying at the University of California, San Diego in the Data Science department, and I plan to graduate during spring of 2023 with a B.S. in Data Science. I am preparing to return to graduate school for a M.S. degree in related fields.

For my study, I specialize in machine learning and ML applications at scale, and this is my professional interest as well. I like crafting data science project involving large scale data processing tools and novel machine learning model architectures. In addition, I have been working on investigating interesting ML applications, such as recommendation systems, graph neural network, etc. I would like to combine more robust ML tools to create data intensive machine learning applications.

If you are interested in my experience and projects, please take a look at my `project` page and `cv`. I try my very best to keep them updated. My contact information is also at the bottom of the page.
